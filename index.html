§ 01 · What this board is

A Pico carrier,
not a bare-RP2040 product yet.

The v0.1 board is a Pico-based carrier. The Pico provides USB, firmware flashing, 3.3V logic, timing, PWM, and the main control loop. The carrier board supplies the cabinet-specific hardware: input expansion, analog conversion, spinner headers, LED drivers, power handling, and readable field wiring.

Control strategy

Digital joysticks and buttons are treated as switch-to-ground inputs. Hall-effect joysticks and potentiometers feed the ADC as voltage axes. Spinners feed the Pico directly as quadrature A/B signals so firmware can convert them to mouse movement or signed deltas.

Software strategy

The board should enumerate as a normal USB input device for gameplay and expose a simple command channel for cabinet feedback. For v0.1, the least painful setup is HID gamepad/mouse for inputs and CDC serial for Unity-driven LEDs.
